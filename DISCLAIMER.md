**This project is not financial advice.**

Hedge Fund at Home is **educational software**. It explains how systematic trading
systems are often organized. It does not provide investment recommendations,
portfolio management, trading signals you should follow, or personalized
financial guidance. Nothing here promises profit or suitability for your goals.

## For education, not real-money trading

The intended use is **learning**:

- **Simulated mode (default):** synthetic fills inside the application. No
  brokerage, no exchange, no real capital.
- **Paper mode (optional):** practice with a broker's paper account if you choose.
  Paper is still not the same as live markets and is not a profit guarantee.
- **Live mode:** exists behind explicit gates only. It is **not** part of the
  documented learning path. Real money can be lost entirely.

**Do not use real money** to work through this repository unless you have your
own independent judgment and professional advice where appropriate. The authors
and contributors are not responsible for your trading decisions.

## Simulated results are hypothetical

Simulated orders do not test liquidity, partial fills, outages, or fees realistically.
Simulated backtests can be wrong through look-ahead bias, overfitting, and bad data.
**Past simulated performance does not predict future results.**
